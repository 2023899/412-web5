For this project, I had to research a topic that would interest you me that I would be able find a lot of content on the web and make a training manual based on the action related to topic that’s quick and easy to understand. I decided to do a manual on how to use FL Studio because I’m and artist and music producer that has been using this digital audio workstation (DAW) for years since I’ve started making music and I wanted to make FL Studio easy to learn so they don’t have to go through the extremely long time of figuring out how to use the software like me when I first started using FL Studio.

Before I started making the manual, I first had to make a flowchart which was an outline of how I was going to approach making the training manual. In the flowchart I had to divide the manual by the segments in order such as the cover page, the license page, intro, index, glossary and the back cover. Under that, I had to write the amount of chapters I needed to have for the manual with the chapter names and under that, I had to write little summaries of what each segment in the chapters were going to be about.
