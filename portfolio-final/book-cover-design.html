Sketches


For this Publication Design assignment, I had to design book covers for three separate books. I had to make three book jackets for the three books that contains the flaps, the spine, the back and front covers. The three books that I decided to make the designed covers of was No New Land by M.G. Vassanji, Jasmine by Bharati Mukherjee and White Tiger by Aravind Adiga.
