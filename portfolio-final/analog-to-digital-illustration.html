Analog to Digital Illustration



For this project, I had to do a sketch on paper, scan it on a printer as a raster graphic and place it into Adobe Illustrator as an embedded graphic. I then trace the sketch using the Pen Tool along with other tools and techniques in order to make a digital vector version of the sketch.

I decided to do my project on Malcolm X and make it a motivational and captivating image with one of his most popular quotes "By Any Means Necessary" with him and he quote in a smooth blue gradient background.
