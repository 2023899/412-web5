For this french project, I had to create an original poster to promote La Semaine de la Francophonie at Vanier College while using a visual metaphor. The main challenge of this project was making the poster not only being an aesthetically pleasing design presenting the information. Instead, I had to create a concept reflecting the theme that can be adapted for different formats and media.

I started this project off by making three sketches so I can have an idea of how I was going to approach this poster. I then picked the sketch that I was going to make my poster revolve around so I scanned the sketch on a printer, sent it to myself, opened up Adobe Ilustrator, started tracing around the sketch and applied the colors. For the visual metaphor, I designed an open book with light rays coming out with floating music, book and movie symbols. This symbolizes that the Semaine de la Francophonie event is going to be about engaging in activitie such as reading, listening to audio or music and watching a video or movie where French is concerned. I made sure to make the poster visually appealing by adding a blue gradient background with simple to read texts.
